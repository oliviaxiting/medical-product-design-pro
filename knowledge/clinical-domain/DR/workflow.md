# DR Clinical Workflow


## Standard Workflow


RIS/HIS

↓

Patient Registration

↓

Protocol Selection

↓

Patient Preparation

↓

Positioning

↓

Exposure Preparation

↓

Exposure

↓

Image Quality Review

↓

Send to PACS



## UX Design Implication


Each step has different information requirements.


Before exposure:

Need:

- patient identity
- body part
- projection
- positioning guidance
- geometry information


During exposure:

Need:

- safety confirmation
- exposure authorization


After exposure:

Need:

- image quality evaluation
- repeat analysis
