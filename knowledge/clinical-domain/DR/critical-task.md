# DR Critical Tasks


Critical tasks are tasks where errors may cause:

- patient harm
- wrong examination
- repeat exposure
- workflow disruption



## Patient Identification

Risk:

Wrong patient imaging


Design:

- Always display patient identity
- Provide confirmation before exposure



## Positioning

Risk:

Incorrect anatomy coverage


Design:

- Provide positioning guidance
- Provide alignment feedback



## Exposure Preparation

Risk:

Wrong parameters


Design:

- Clearly separate recommendation and authorization
- Never hide exposure status
