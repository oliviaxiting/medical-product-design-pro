# Dashboard Review

## Purpose

This document defines review rules for dashboards used in medical Console and PAD applications.

The objective is to improve situational awareness and support rapid clinical decision-making.

---

## Human Factors Principle

Dashboards should provide immediate understanding of system status without requiring detailed exploration.

Users should recognize abnormal conditions within seconds.

---

## HE75 Recommendations

### Information Hierarchy

Display the most clinically important information first.

Secondary information should not compete for attention.

---

### Overview First

Provide a clear overview before showing detailed information.

Support progressive disclosure.

---

### Grouping

Group information by:

- Patient
- Device
- Workflow
- Clinical meaning

Avoid arbitrary grouping.

---

### Visual Consistency

Maintain consistent cards, spacing, icons, and labels.

Avoid unnecessary visual variety.

---

### Trend Visibility

Whenever possible, combine current values with trends.

Current values alone may be insufficient.

---

### Status Visibility

Critical status should always remain visible.

Do not hide abnormal conditions inside secondary pages.

---

## Console Application

Recommendations:

- Multi-patient overview.
- Persistent monitoring.
- Quick navigation.
- Large-screen optimization.
- Multiple information panels.

---

## PAD Application

Recommendations:

- Current patient first.
- Task-oriented dashboard.
- Simplified layout.
- Minimal interaction.

---

## Common Risks

- Information overload.
- Equal visual weight.
- Missing hierarchy.
- Hidden abnormalities.
- Decorative dashboards.

---

## Design Review Checklist

Before approving the design, verify:

□ Important information appears first.

□ Dashboard supports rapid scanning.

□ Trends are visible.

□ Critical status remains visible.

□ Console supports monitoring.

□ PAD supports bedside review.

---

## AI Review Rules

Always evaluate:

- Situational awareness
- Information hierarchy
- Scan efficiency
- Clinical relevance

Never prioritize visual symmetry over clinical priority.
